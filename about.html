About Artists' Reverie

Born from a Dream

Artists' Reverie was founded by a small collective of artists who believed that the tools you create with should feel as beautiful as the work itself.

Given the art supplies, chosen for their quality for yours to create with, be it a personal creation.

Whether you're a seasoned painter or someone who just started sketching on weekends, this is your space.

✦

Quality First

Every product is tested by real artists. If it doesn't feel right in hand, it doesn't make it onto our shelves.

◇

Thoughtfully Curated

We don't carry everything — just the things we'd personally reach for on a quiet creative afternoon.

○

Made with Care

Many of our pieces are sourced from small studios and independent makers who pour their craft into every detail.
